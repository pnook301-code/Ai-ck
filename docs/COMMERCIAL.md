# CK-NEXUS AIOS — Commercial Guide

## Product Overview

CK-NEXUS AIOS is an Enterprise AI Operating System designed for businesses that need to deploy, manage, and scale AI capabilities across their organization.

## Target Market

- **Enterprise:** Large organizations needing AI infrastructure
- **SaaS Companies:** Building AI-powered products
- **Agencies:** Providing AI services to clients
- **Startups:** Needing enterprise-grade AI without building from scratch
